About South Africa

South Africa, officially the Republic of South Africa, is a country in the southern parts of Africa that borders with Botswana, Lesotho, Mozambique, Namibia, Eswatini and Zimbabwe.

Technologically and industrially, its one of the most advanced countries in south Africa and is the continents second largest economy which reflects that.

South Africa has 11 official languages reflecting the incredible multicultural focus they have.

South Africa is a member of the UN, the AU, the CoN, the G20 and the SADC.

South AFrica is a middle-income somewhat emerging market which is incredibly wealthy in natural resources, but without the ties needed to make the money they can. They're believed to rapidly rise to the top in near future.

South Africa is still ridden with poverty and inequality. A quarter of the population is unemployed and living on less than 15kr a day,

South Africa is a member of BRICS along with Brazil, Russia, India and China.

Transportation in South Africa

Use MyCiTi Bus — Best Public Option

Public transport is highly reliable and safe in SA, though you need a myconnect card that you must fill up with money. It operates on a set schedule and has designated public transport lanes which works great for rush hour.
