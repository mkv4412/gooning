Cape Town Attractions

Discover the best Cape Town has to offer with these amazing attractions and tours. From iconic Table Mountain to thrilling adventures, there's something for everyone in the Mother City.

Top Attractions with Details

Table Mountain Cableway

Opening Hours: Daily 8:00AM-6:00PM (weather dependent)

Entrance Fee: Adults R395 return, Children R200, Students R315

Description: Iconic flat-topped mountain offering 360° views of Cape Town. Cable car rotates during 5-minute journey to summit.

Tips: Book online to avoid queues, check weather conditions, bring warm clothes for summit.

Robben Island Museum

Opening Hours: Daily tours at 9:00AM, 11:00AM, 1:00PM, 3:00PM

Entrance Fee: Adults R320, Students/Pensioners R200, Children (under 18) R160

Description: UNESCO World Heritage Site where Nelson Mandela was imprisoned. Includes ferry ride and guided tour by former political prisoner.

Tips: Book weeks in advance, bring ID, tour lasts 3.5 hours total including ferry.

V&A Waterfront

Opening Hours: Daily 9:00AM-11:00PM (shops/restaurants vary)

Entrance Fee: Free to enter (individual attractions charge separately)

Description: Historic harbor with shopping, dining, entertainment, and attractions like Two Oceans Aquarium and Zeitz Museum.

Tips: Allow full day, great for sunset, Two Oceans Aquarium R235 adults.

Boulders Penguin Colony

Opening Hours: Daily 8:00AM-5:00PM (March-April until 6:30PM)

Entrance Fee: Adults R152, Children R76, SA residents R76/R38

Description: Home to over 2,000 African penguins in their natural habitat. Protected boardwalks for viewing.

Tips: Early morning or late afternoon for best viewing, combine with Cape Point visit.

Cape Point Nature Reserve

Opening Hours: Daily 6:00AM-6:00PM (April-September until 5:00PM)

Entrance Fee: Adults R390, Children R195, SA residents R75/R40

Description: Dramatic cliffs, lighthouse, and the famous "Cape of Good Hope" sign. Part of Table Mountain National Park.

Tips: Drive scenic Chapman's Peak, funicular to lighthouse R80 return, baboons present - don't feed!

Kirstenbosch Botanical Gardens

Opening Hours: Daily 8:00AM-6:00PM (April-August until 6:00PM)

Entrance Fee: Adults R85, Students/Pensioners R40, Children (under 6) Free

Description: World-renowned botanical garden showcasing South African flora. Tree Canopy Walkway and outdoor concerts.

Tips: Sunday concerts in summer (Nov-April), Tree Canopy Walkway additional R40.

Planning Tips

Book popular attractions like Table Mountain and Robben Island in advance, especially during peak season (December-February). Consider combination tours to save time and money.

Weather can change quickly in Cape Town, so bring layers and check conditions before outdoor activities like paragliding or helicopter tours.
